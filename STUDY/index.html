The Hotel Green was a hotel in Pasadena, California.

It was built in 1893 by George Gill Green, and later expanded by him with two additional buildings

in 1898 and 1903, creating a complex of three structures. The Hotel Green was

the home of the Valley Hunt Club and the Tournament of Roses association
